# Pricing Unit

The Pricing Unit represents a pricing measurement unit, which forms the foundation for determining unit prices of utilizing a particular resource and/or service and subsequently calculating the corresponding costs.  Though it can be used for quantifying the usage, its primary function lies in clarifying understanding of the pricing aspect.

## Column ID

PricingUnit

## Display name

Pricing Unit

## Description

Pricing unit is the unit of measure that providers use to calculate the cost of goods and services.
